Our Biosphere

The biosphere is the global ecological system integrating all living beings and their relationships. This includes their interaction with the elements of the lithosphere, geosphere, hydrosphere, and atmosphere.

Scientific Definitions

The biosphere is a term coined by geologist Eduard Suess, meaning the zone of life on Earth, encompassing all ecosystems.

Elements of Biosphere

  • Atmosphere
  • Hydrosphere
  • Lithosphere
  • Ecosystems
